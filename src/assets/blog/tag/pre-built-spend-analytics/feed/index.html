The truth is analytics solutions are hard to build from scratch. Business users need to imagine the usage and think through all scenarios without seeing anything (difficult), availability of internal resources with skills in data science with business acumen, data extract, transform and load (ETL) (challenging), rationalizing business requirement and creating compelling metrics (hard), designing intuitive dashboard (challenging).

Of the shelf analytic applications integrate all the components required to deliver value in quickest possible time. Pre-built analytics are cheaper and quicker to implement and easier to maintain. Pre-built solutions such as DecisionSigma contain industry best practices and techniques and have proven value in other organizations. DecisionSigma comes with plugins for major ERPs such as SAP and Oracle and an enterprise data model. This makes it even easier to integrate with existing internal systems and realize return on investment (ROI) quicker.

 Analyze raw material price volatility and simulate profitability
 Gain deep insights into Rate, Volume, Mix & FX impact

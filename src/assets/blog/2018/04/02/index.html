Why Procurement Visibility & Spend Analytics Are Important for Organizations?

As CFOs face growing margin pressure, they look for options to cut costs. Because procurement team is in charge of reducing raw material prices and costs, and identifying better sources of supply, CFOs turn to procurement team to provide new source of cost optimization, ease the margin pressure and improve the ‘bottom line’.
To meet growing expectation from shareholders, over the last few years procurement function has put a lot of attention to organizational development, more centralized way of working and standardization in procurement’s operating model. Therefore, more than ever, procurement teams need actionable insights from their data to improve performance in their day to day operations. Starting from analyze consumption patterns to options to move towards bundled purchase to purchasing more from preferred suppliers to creating effective category plans, procurement leadership team is looking for every options to reduce cost.
While it sounds obvious, many companies have procurement teams who are still crunching several spread sheets or using intuition to forecast price and measure impact. Few companies have started analytics journey, but competing priorities, time required building analytics from scratch and lack of internal expertise slows their progress.
If you haven’t started analytics journey, now is the time to start. If you have already started and looking for ways to expedite deployment, then you need to look for pre-packaged solutions that can expedite implementation and enable you quickly mature in your analytics capabilities. Strengthening procurement analytics capabilities would help you dramatically improve organizational performance, enable you to provide new opportunities for cost saving and enhance your ability to create strategic impact on your business.
